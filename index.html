Foundation

Week 1 - Week 3

This stage is your orientation: meeting your cohort mates, understanding, the history of design & getting your mindset and habits right. Learn the fundamentals of visual design & Figma & get thrown head-first into your first design project. Receive feedback & iterate.

Procces

Week - 4 - Week 6

Beyond fundamentals, it's now time for framework. Understanding the design procces, mental models, research, metrics and product. A mix of case studies & in-depth assignment to apply theory to practice.

Deep Skills

Week - 7 - Week 9

With project and procces under your belt, get in to the depths of motion design and prototyping tools. Learn how to polish your visuals, advanced prototyping in ProtoPie, making website in Webflow and more. Test your skills with two design assigment

Graduation

Week - 10 - Week 12

Conceptualize your finals capstone project to apply everything you've learn. This stage also foccused on personal brand, preparing case studies with your projects so far and how to start finding opportunities - freelance, intership & full time roles.

Acceleration

Week 13 onwards...

Congrats, you're designer now! This duration of the program will be foccused on polishing your work and getting your opportunities. All members will receive interview prep, dedicated placement assistnace and coaching personally from me.

Frequently asked question

If you have any question about the program that aren't covered here, email me at gilangpradnyana1@agmail.com and i'll get in touch

Who is this course for?

This course is for thoose who want to learn UI/UX design to transition into a new career or to build an additional skill. Wether you're a student, engineer, product manager, quality control or anyone else

How do i apply for the course?

The course has limited seats, so please take some time to fill out your application and answer the questions to your bes ability. There might be an interview call with me to assess if you're ready for the program.

How much will the course cost overall?

The couse wil cost $75 (IDR.1.224.000) wich needs to be paid upfront before the start of the course. There are also 3mo, 6mo and 12mo EMI options avilable offered by Paypall.

How much time do i need to dedicate every week?

The curriculum has been design to allow people participate part-time. However you must be able to dedicate a minimum of 5-6 hours every week based on the work to watch the session and submit your assignment on time.

Can i do this course at my own pace?

The course requires you to watch the live classes and make assignment on time every week. So no, the course can't be done at your pace

What if i miss a live session?

You are encouraged to watch the session live to keep up with the curriculum but if you're unable to attend, class recordings are available for every class.

Will i be guaranteed a job after graduating the course?

The training and community is designed to increase your odds of getting roles. I can't guarantee success but you will receive personal coaching from me that will help you build a portofolio and confidently apply for internship, freelane and full-time roles

Can i cancel or apply for a refund?

If you not happy with the course within the first 7 days, you will receive a full refund.

Will i get a certificate on completing the course?

Yes, you will receive a certificate on completion of the course. However, the skills you build are the what will unlock opportunities for you, not for the certificate.

Where are the classes conducted?

All course instruction will be online and all you need is an internet connection to watch the videos. However there might be physical meetups post COVID, wich are optional to attend and the cost will have to be borne by you.

What computer specs and software for I need for the course?

You need a Mac or Windows computer capable of running a web browse. Figma will be the primary application in this course.

Do I need to know programming?

No, you don't need to know programming or be a Science or Computer Science graduated to do this course. The web development you will learn will be using WebFLow. a no-code visual development tool.

Can i apply if im a Student?

Yes, you can apply even if you're school or college student. Your focus will be to learn how to apply for internship and freelance project.

How do i know if UI/UX design is right for me

UI/UX design might be the choiche for you if you're interested in the intersection of design, technology and business. You want to work in the tech industry and want to build products for web and mobile. Check out my free videos on Instagram.
